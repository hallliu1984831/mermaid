### The Time Gap Between Wealth Creation vs Wealth Distribution

Altman's prophecy has a fatal flaw: he assumed wealth creation and wealth distribution would happen simultaneously.

Reality is:
- Wealth creation: Explosive growth (252.5 billion investment)
- Wealth distribution: Still in "growing pains" (massive layoffs)

This time gap is the dilemma ordinary people face now.

### AI's "Substitution Effect" vs "Creation Effect"

Economics tells us new technology produces two effects:
1. Substitution effect: Machines replace human labor, reducing employment
2. Creation effect: New technology creates new jobs, increasing employment

Currently, the substitution effect comes faster and more fiercely:
- Customer service, data entry, simple design jobs are disappearing in large numbers
- While new positions like AI engineers and data scientists require high-skill barriers

Ordinary people are caught in the middle: old jobs are disappearing, new jobs are out of reach.
